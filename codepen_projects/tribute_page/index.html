Patrick Daniel Tillman

From NFL Player to United States Army Ranger

Time Line of Pat Tillman's Life:

  • 1976 - Born in San Jose, California
  • 1994 - Started college on a football scholarship with Arizona State University
  • 1996 & 1997 - Earned the Clyde B. Smith Academic Award
  • 1997 - Earned the Sporting News Honda Scholar-Athlete of the Year
  • 1998 - Won the Sun Angel Student Athlete of the Year
  • 1998 - Selected in the 7th round of the NFL draft by the Arizona Cardinals
  • 2002 - Turned downed a $3.6 million dollar renewal contract with the Cardinals
  • 2002 - Pat Tillman and his brother Kevin Tillman enlisted in the U.S. Army
  • 2003 - Deployed to Iraq in part of Operation Iraqi Freedom
  • 2003 - Graduated Ranger School
  • 2004 - Deployed to Afghanistan in part of Operation Enduring Freedom
  • 2004 - Died near Sperah, Afghanistan
  • 2010 - Posthumously inducted into the College Football Hall of Fame
  • 2018 - Posthumously inducted into the Arizona Sports Hall of Fame

"Nothing can bring you peace but yourself. Nothing can bring you peace but the triumph of principles."

Patrick Daniel Tillman
